NP Membership

Designed for non-profit organizations, the NP Membership offers a unique blend of community support and resources. Enjoy access to networking events, educational workshops, and promotional opportunities on our community bulletin. This tier is tailored to help non-profits thrive, providing a platform to connect, collaborate, and contribute to the local economy without any membership fees.

Bronze Membership

Our Bronze Membership is the perfect starting point for small businesses seeking to establish their presence in the local market. Members at this level receive a listing in our online business directory, access to monthly networking events, and the opportunity to participate in member-only training sessions. Bronze members also benefit from discounted rates for chamber-sponsored workshops and events, providing a solid foundation for business growth and community engagement.

Silver Membership

Step up to Silver Membership and unlock additional benefits designed to amplify your business's reach. This tier includes all Bronze benefits plus enhanced visibility through featured spots in our newsletter and on our website. Silver members also gain access to exclusive business development seminars and have the opportunity to host chamber events, showcasing their business to a broader audience. This membership is ideal for businesses looking to expand their network and influence in the local community.

Gold Membership

The pinnacle of our membership tiers, Gold Membership, is designed for businesses seeking premier services and recognition. Gold members enjoy priority placement in all chamber publications and direct referrals from the chamber to potential clients. This tier also includes exclusive access to high-level networking events, round-table discussions with community leaders, and opportunities for joint marketing campaigns. As a Gold member, your business becomes a distinguished partner of the Chamber, leading the way in community development and innovation.
